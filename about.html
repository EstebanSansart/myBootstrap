About Us:

SANSART STUDIOS

Sansart Studios is a company that develops indie video games. It was founded in Colombia and has had a relatively short journey so far. Currently, we are in charge of maintaining the game "Solsticea" with constant content so that our players feel comfortable during their stay here. Soon, we will have new projects that will see the light of day!
The creator of Solsticea is Esteban Sansar, a software programmer who started his career in his hometown. Currently, there are more people working with him, and together they will develop a massive virtual universe that will revolutionize the industry.
